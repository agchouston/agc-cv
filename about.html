About Us

As a local trade organization, AGC Houston has been representing the best interests of the commercial construction industry of Greater Houston for more than 95 years. While a representative of the industry at large, the association is supported by a membership of over 550 firms. This includes general and specialty contractors, as well as service and supply companies. These firms employ over 27,000 individuals in the region.

The Voice of the Industry

Since 1923, AGC Houston has been supporting the commercial construction industry in a 24-county area in and around the Greater Houston region.

Government Affairs & Advocacy

Through its Government Affairs Committee (GAC) the chapter is dedicated to staying abreast of the regulatory, legal and political issues that affect our members at both the state and local level. As a rule AGC Houston does not endorse political candidates. The GAC does make recommendations to the AGC Houston PAC, which actively supports candidates whose interests closely align to what is important to the chapter and its members.
